About Me

Hi! I am a second year PhD student at Chennai Mathematical Institute, India working under the supervision of Prof. C. Aiswarya and Prof. Prakash Saivasan.

My interests broadly lie in Formal verification. I am currently working on the verification of String Constraints. I am also interested in Infinite State Systems and Concurrency Theory.
